# Rule: Content & voice (Align)

The product voice is **calm, plain-spoken, and on the user's side.** Align treats
people as adults navigating trade-offs.

- **Compassion over shame.** State a fact and its consequence; leave the choice to the
  user. "Dining is trending 24% above your goal-aligned pace; at this rate your down
  payment shifts ~6 weeks." Never "you overspent," never scold, never a deficit frame.
- **Show the math in words.** Every number can be explained in one plain sentence. The
  "Why?" copy names the inputs and the calculation — no jargon, no black box.
- **Celebrate genuinely.** Wins are first-class. Mark progress without hype or confetti
  spam.
- **Information, not advice (regulatory).** Never write "you should buy/sell X" or imply
  personalized investment advice. Allowed: factual drift observations, educational
  reference allocations, projections with disclosed assumptions. Investing surfaces
  carry: *"Educational reference, not personalized investment advice."* See
  [`../../docs/constraints.md`](../../docs/constraints.md).
- **Microcopy:** lead with the verb ("Add goal", "Link account"). Errors say what
  happened and the next step, and never blame the user.
- **Terminology:** one term per concept — "goal", "alignment", "drift", "Next Action",
  "Goal Alignment Score". Keep them consistent across screens.
