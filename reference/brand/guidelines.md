# Brand guidelines — Align

The brand expresses **calm and trust** — deliberately between institutional
blue-and-serif fintech and aggressive neobank gradients.

- **Name & voice:** "Align." Calm, plain-spoken, on the user's side. Full voice in
  [`../../.claude/rules/content-voice.md`](../../.claude/rules/content-voice.md).
- **Color:** warm sand surface `#FAF7F2`, teal brand `#2C7A7B`, navy headlines
  `#0F2A4A`. Maps to product tokens in
  [`../../design-tokens.json`](../../design-tokens.json). No second brand color, no
  gradients.
- **Typography:** Inter (400/500/600/700); tabular figures for currency. No serifs, no
  enterprise blue.
- **Motion & imagery:** restrained, eased (200–280ms). Imagery, when used, is warm and
  human, never stock-photo "happy at a laptop." No confetti spam for celebrations.
- **Logo:** TBD — drop assets in [`logo/`](./logo/) with clearspace and min-size specs.
